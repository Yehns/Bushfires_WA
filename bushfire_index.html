Bushfires in WA

Bushfire Data

    The DBCA Fire History dataset is a collection of records of fire events (controlled burns and bushfires) that occurred on land managed by the Western Australian Department of Biodiversity, Conservation and Attractions, and sometimes on non-department-managed land. The dataset includes information from completed projects, such as the Great Western Woodlands project and the Gnangara Sustainability Strategy project, which used remote sensing and other methods to digitize fire events and verify fire records. The dataset also includes information on areas of clear-felled plantation and mining rehabilitation.
